Road Reserve Mapping — KERRA Projects

As part of my work with the Kenya Rural Roads Authority (KeRRA), I was involved in the mapping and delineation of road reserves using ArcGIS and Civil 3D. These projects focused on accurately defining the spatial extents of existing and proposed road corridors, supporting land acquisition, planning, and compliance with regulatory standards. Leveraging geospatial datasets and design tools, I produced clear, data-backed reserve maps that informed sustainable infrastructure development..

Conclusion

Through the integration of ArcGIS for geospatial analysis and Civil 3D for design visualization, these road reserve mapping projects have played a vital role in enhancing planning accuracy and ensuring legal compliance in infrastructure development. The outputs supported KeRRA’s mandate to improve rural accessibility while minimizing land disputes and optimizing corridor usage. These projects reflect my ability to apply technical tools to solve real-world infrastructure challenges with precision and impact.
