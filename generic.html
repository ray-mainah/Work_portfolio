MONITORING WATER POLLUTION IN LAKE NAIVASHA

This project uses ArcGIS and Google Earth Engine to analyze how fertilizer runoff from flower farms impacts Lake Naivasha's water quality. Satellite data and indices like NDVI and NDCI were used to map pollution trends over time. The results show a clear connection between agricultural activity and shoreline degradation.

NDVI-LST ANALYSIS OF NAIROBI: EXPLORING URBAN HEAT
AND VEGETATION WITH GEE & PYTHON

This project explores the spatial relationship between vegetation cover and urban heat in Nairobi, Kenya, using satellite data, Google Earth Engine, and Python-based analysis. The analysis includes correlation mapping, thematic visualizations, and optional sub-county-level zonal statistics offering valuable insights for urban sustainability and climate adaptation planning.

  • Open maps

TURKANA COUNTY WATER QUALITY MAP (Fluoride & pH)

This project visualizes water quality indicators; fluoride, pH, and electrical conductivity across Turkana County, Kenya. Built using Python and Folium, the interactive web map highlights unsafe water sources, overlays the Turkana boundary, and helps communicate public health risks through geospatial analysis.
